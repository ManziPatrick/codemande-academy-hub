# About CODEMANDE

CODEMANDE is a premier technology institution based in Kigali, Rwanda, dedicated to fostering innovation and excellence in the African tech ecosystem.

## Our Story
Founded by tech visionaries Manzi Alain Patrick and Bizimana Eric, CODEMANDE began as a mission to solve the local skills gap in Rwanda. Today, it stands as a bridge between talented individuals and the global tech industry.

## Leadership
- **Manzi Alain Patrick Munyeshuri**: Founder & CEO. Expert in digital transformation and strategic growth.
- **Bizimana Eric**: Co-Founder & CTO. Lead architect with expertise in AI, Cloud, and Software Engineering.

## Our Core Services
1. **Academy**: Practical, job-ready training in FullStack Dev, AI, and IoT.
2. **Software House**: Building robust custom software for local and international clients.
3. **IoT Lab**: Developing smart hardware solutions for agriculture and smart cities.

## Mission
To empower African youth with high-end tech skills and build software that solves real-world problems.

## Vision
To lead Africa's digital revolution through quality education and innovative technology.
